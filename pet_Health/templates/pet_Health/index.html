Welcome to the Pet Health Portal

Pets can add fun, companionship, and a feeling of safety to your life. Before getting a pet, think carefully about which animal is best for your family.

Consider factors like:

  • What each family member wants in a pet
  • Who will take care of it
  • Pet allergies (if any)
  • Lifestyle and budget compatibility

Once you own a pet, keep it healthy. Know the signs of medical problems. Take your pet to the veterinarian if you notice:

  • Loss of appetite
  • Drinking a lot of water
  • Gaining or losing a lot of weight quickly
  • Strange behavior
  • Being sluggish and tired
  • Trouble getting up or down
  • Strange lumps
